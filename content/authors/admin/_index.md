Alex Milward is a Master's Candidate at the Bren School of Environmental Science & Management at the University of California, Santa Barbara. His research interests include distributed robotics, mobile computing and programmable matter. He leads the Robotic Neurobiology group, which develops self-reconfiguring robots, systems of self-organizing robots, and mobile sensor networks.
